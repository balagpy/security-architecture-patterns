# Contributing

Contributions are welcome if they meet the repository's research quality bar.

## Contribution Types

- new architecture case studies
- expanded mitigation analysis
- improved diagrams and threat models
- reproducible and safe lab simulations

## Submission Standard

Every case-study folder should include:
- `README.md`
- `architecture.svg`
- `attack-flow.svg`
- `mitigations.md`
- `references.md`

Please align with [METHODOLOGY.md](./METHODOLOGY.md) before opening a PR.
